FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is ZentraFuel (ZFUEL)?
ZentraFuel is a deflationary utility token on Base blockchain. With a fixed 1M supply, burnable tokenomics, holder tier rewards, and community governance - it's designed for long-term value growth.
Is this legit? How can I verify?
100% legit and transparent. Our contract is verified on Basescan - anyone can read the code. The founder is accessible (not anonymous). Check the contract yourself at basescan.org.
What's the utility beyond trading?
Multiple layers: Staking rewards for passive income, governance voting rights, holder tier benefits (Bronze to Diamond), token-gated community access, and deflationary burns that reduce supply over time.
How do the holder tiers work?
Hold more ZFUEL, unlock more perks. Bronze (1K+) gets community access. Silver (5K+) gets voting rights. Gold (25K+) gets 2x staking rewards. Diamond (100K+) gets 3x rewards and VIP access.
What are the burn events?
Every Sunday at 12:00 UTC, we hold community burns. Tokens are permanently destroyed, reducing total supply. Goal: Cut supply from 1M to 500K. Less supply = more scarcity = potential value increase.
Why is ZFUEL on Base?
Base is Coinbase's Layer 2 blockchain. Benefits: Gas fees under $0.01, 2-3 second transactions, Ethereum-level security, growing ecosystem, and institutional backing from Coinbase.
How do I contact the founder?
DMs are open on Twitter @ZentraFuel. I respond to all genuine questions. Not an anonymous team - real person, real project, real accountability.
