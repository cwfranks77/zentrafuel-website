How to Buy ZFUEL

Get started in 4 simple steps

1

Get a Wallet

Download MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet. These are free browser extensions or mobile apps that let you store crypto.

2

Add Base Network

Add the Base network to your wallet. Chain ID: 8453. Or use Coinbase Wallet which has it built-in.

3

Get ETH on Base

Buy ETH and bridge it to Base, or buy directly on Base through Coinbase. You need ETH to pay for gas fees.

4

Swap for ZFUEL

Go to Uniswap, connect your wallet, paste the ZFUEL token address, and swap your ETH for ZFUEL!
